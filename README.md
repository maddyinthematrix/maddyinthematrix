## About

Cybersecurity researcher and final-stage student focused on the intersection of **technical security**, **artificial intelligence**, and **human behavior**. My work spans vulnerability research, AI security (including the OWASP Top 10 for AI), and the psychological dimensions of cyber risk — from social engineering to criminal psychology. I build security tooling, contribute to open-source research, and speak regularly on cyber awareness and emerging-tech topics.
